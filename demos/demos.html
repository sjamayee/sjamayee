Demo Description
Splitter Splitter demo.
There should be only one active vertical split (Top(List/Grid) - Bottom(ToolBar+Detail)), the other splitters should be disabled.
Splitting should be fixed or variable with (initial, minimum and maximum sizes).
Header and Toolbar panes should be of fixed size.
Panes should be resizable in both directions (width and height).
The result should be a full size, resizable grid, with small grid of a few lines and more lines in the property panes or a maximum grid with many lines and only a few lines in the property panes.
